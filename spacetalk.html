About Spacetalk

Spacetalk is a mobile phone built into a smartwatch designed just for kids aged 5 to 12. Spacetalk allows two-way phone calls and SMS messaging for children to a parent-controlled list of contacts, and GPS tracking alerts parents whenever children leave designated safe spaces, such as school or home.

The company is now recognised as a global leader and pioneer in socially responsible wearable technology for families. Everything we do is built on our unshakeable desire for families to feel safe and secure so that they can live with freedom, independence and joy.

Working as the sole designer, and later leading a team of 4 designers, I had the pleasure of owning the customer experience across the spacetalk brand and its devices. Collaborating cross-functionally with the founder, product and engineering some of the highlights of my tenure were:

  • Designed, supported manufacturing and developed art direction and marketing, for 2 award-winning wearable devices.
    Led design consultation for C-level executives, producing 3d and CAD designs, packaging materials, digital branding collateral and multimedia marketing assets.
  • Developed a robust multi-platform design system for wearable devices, mobile apps (iOS & Android) and the web.
    Facilitated user testing and research using lean methodology, to develop components for a new category of devices while retaining brand familiarity and ease of use.
  • Released a fresh rebrand for the company.
    Designed a new look and feel, with guidelines to enable spacetalk and its brand partners to provide a consistent experience.
  • Led product design and implemented the user experience for spacetalk's AI fall detection feature.
    Working closely with the CEO and engineering lead, I developed a fall detection feature for the seniors' watch.
